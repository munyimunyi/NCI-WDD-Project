Clean Water and Sanitation - Local Community Participation

Target 6.6 (Local Community Participation) aims to support and strengthen participation of local communities by improving water and sanitaion management. The indicatior 6.b.1 checks whether local authorities have clear laws, policies and procedures which allow the local communities to be involved with the planning and management of water and sanitation services.

Real participation means that the locals can help find problems, choose solutions and monitor the results to see if they are effective, rather than only being informed after the decisions have been made. Involving the local communities can lead to projects (such as new water pipes and public taps) being better maintained and can be made sustainably.
